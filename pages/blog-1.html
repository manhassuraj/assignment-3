Leonardo AI: Explore the depths your creativity

Posted on: April 6, 2024

Read more about Leonardo AI on this article.

Leonardo AI is image generation and editing platform that allows users to create highly imaginative and visually stunning images. The platform provides an intuitive image generation tool where user input text prompts to describe the desired image, and the AI will generate multiple variations. Beyond just generating images, Leonardo AI also offers advanced editing features through its Canvas Editor. This allows users to make detailed adjustments, erase elements, and even generate new content through inpainting and outpainting techniques.The platform also provides tools like Image Guidance, MultiControlNets, and Prompt Magic v3 to give users fine-grained control othe image generation and editing process.
